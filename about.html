City Sync - your city guide

Who Are We?

The main goal of our project is to solve the problem of information fragmentation for both visitors and locals. As exchange students, we have learned from our own experience the difficulty of finding authentic local events without being deeply involved in pre-existing social circles or specific social media groups. The information is scattered across dozens of platforms: university forums, little-known Facebook groups, coffee shop flyers, and word of mouth. CitySync aims to be a centralized, reliable, and easy-to-use platform that brings these disparate events together. This will help users transform from passive observers into active participants in their community, lowering the barrier to detection.
