Ciné-Club SVĚT NARUBY

10 October – 1 November

Ciné-Club Pomezí, in collaboration with Alliance française de Brno, invites you to a new series of well-known and lesser-known French films under the theme THE WORLD UPSIDE DOWN. Come enjoy and discuss these turbulent artistic films. Starting at 7 PM.

Admission: 100 CZK / 80 CZK
10/9 – Paris Again
A film about a terrorist attack in Paris, but above all about what happens to a person after surviving such an event.
08/10 – Paris Sleeps
A hundred-year-old sci-fi imagining what it would be like if all of Paris, except for a few, suddenly fell asleep.
12/11 – Shattered World
A story about a teenager whose world suddenly collapses. Starring Juliette Binoche.
10/12 – Crime on the Express
A crime thriller set on the Marseille–Paris train. Starring Yves Montand and Simone Signoret.

Location

Kino Ponrepo
Bartolomějská 11, Prague 1

Admission

Adults – 100 CZK
Students – 80 CZK
